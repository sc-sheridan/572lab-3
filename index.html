Geology Hall Walking Tour

Welcome to Weeks Hall of Geological Sciences in Madison, WI! Located just down the road from Camp Randall and Union South it is the home to geology and geologic engineering majors! It has a deep history, one that predates the building itself. Originally located in Science Hall the Geoscience department has grown and changed throughout the years. This is apparent in many ways in the current building. From fossils donated to the University of Wisconsin-Madison in the 1890s to the courtyard rocks with surprising backstories from recent years there is much to learn about this wonderful building. So, let’s take a walk through the history of UW Geology!
